    Standard door-open symbol, exclude plumbing markers from column detection,
    and add exhaustive grid-search fallback to the DXF export safety net
    
    - Door symbol was a closed pie (leaf-arc-leaf), reading as a wedge slice.
      Standard CAD convention is just the door leaf (hinge to open position)
      plus the swing arc, with no line closing back to the hinge.
    - detect_columns()'s geometry-only CIRCLE branch had no lower size floor
      beyond 50mm, so small water inlet/outlet marker pairs were also being
      classified as round columns. Raised the geometry-only minimum to 250mm
      (named-layer columns are unaffected).
    - The DXF export's polygon safety net only tried 8 centroid-fraction nudge
      steps, which can still fail for an irregular polygon. Added an exhaustive
      200mm grid search over the whole store as a final fallback so a fixture
      is never rendered outside the real wall.
